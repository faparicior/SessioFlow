# AI ADR Audit


**Role**: Act as a Senior Software Architect and review the following Architectural Decision Record (ADR), which has been filled out using the **Architectural Decision Record Framework Template**.

**Your Goal**: Provide a critical, constructive assessment to ensure the ADR is high-quality, complete, and unbiased before socialisation.

## Validation Criteria

Evaluate the ADR against the following checklist:

### 1. Metadata & Formal Compliance
*   **Header Completeness**: Are `Status`, `Date`, and `Decision Makers` filled in?
*   **Status Validity**: Is the status one of: `Proposed`, `Accepted`, `Rejected`, `Deprecated`, or `Superseded`?
*   **Version Control**: Are `Supersedes` and `Amended By` fields present (even if "N/A")?

### 2. Context & Problem Statement
*   **Clarity**: Is the problem statement clear, focusing on the "what" and "why" rather than the "how"?
*   **Value-Neutral**: Is the language objective and free of bias towards a pre-determined solution?
*   **Decision Drivers**: Are specific constraints (business, technical, legal) explicitly listed as `decision drivers`?

### 3. Options & Analysis
*   **Variety**: Are at least 2-3 viable options considered? (Avoid "Strawman" options).
*   **Pros & Cons**: Does the `Pros and Cons of the Options` section provide a balanced view for *each* option?
*   **Depth**: Are the arguments technically sound and relevant to the context?

### 4. Decision Outcome
*   **Clear Choice**: Is the `Chosen Option` clearly stated?
*   **Justification**: Does the justification logically follow from the `Decision Drivers` and the Pros/Cons analysis?

### 5. Consequences
*   **Completeness**: Does the ADR explicitly list **Positive**, **Negative**, and **Risks**?
*   **Honesty**: Are negative consequences (e.g., increased complexity, technical debt) acknowledged?

### 6. Links & References
*   Are references to ticket systems (Jira) or external docs included where applicable?
