# Step 3: Personas

## Primary Persona: Conference Organizer

### Profile
- **Name**: Sarah Chen
- **Role**: Academic Conference Program Chair
- **Age**: 42
- **Organization**: University Research Department
- **Tech Savviness**: Moderate

### Goals & Motivations
- Streamline the submission and review process
- Reduce administrative overhead
- Ensure fair and unbiased reviewer assignment
- Generate professional schedules quickly

### Frustrations & Pain Points
- Currently managing 200+ submissions via email and spreadsheets
- Manual reviewer assignment leads to conflicts (authors reviewing their own work)
- Schedule creation takes days of manual work
- No visibility into review progress

### Behavior Patterns
- Prefers web-based tools that require no installation
- Values clear workflows and status tracking
- Needs to coordinate with multiple committee members
- Works under tight deadlines (typical conference timeline: 3-4 months)

### Quote
> "I spend more time managing the submission process than actually reviewing papers. I need something that just works."
