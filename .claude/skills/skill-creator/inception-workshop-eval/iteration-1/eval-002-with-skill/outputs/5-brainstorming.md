3. Submission status tracking (submitted, under review, accepted, rejected)
4. Bulk import from CSV
5. Submission templates for different tracks
6. Duplicate submission detection
7. Submission editing before deadline
8. Submission versioning

10. Automated reviewer assignment
11. Conflict of interest detection
12. Reviewer capacity management
13. Reviewer invitation and onboarding

24. Review anonymization

### Communication
