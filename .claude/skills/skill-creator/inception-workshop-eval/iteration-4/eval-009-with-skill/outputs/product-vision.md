# Step 1: Product Vision & Boundaries - EcoTrack

## Elevator Pitch

| For | Who | The Product | Is a | That | Unlike | Our Product |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Environmentally conscious individuals and small business owners | Want to understand and reduce their carbon footprint | EcoTrack | A mobile and web application | Tracks, analyzes, and provides actionable insights to reduce carbon emissions | Generic carbon calculators and spreadsheets | EcoTrack provides automated tracking, personalized recommendations, and progress visualization |

## Product Goals

1. **Goal 1**: Help users reduce their carbon footprint by 20% within 6 months of using EcoTrack
2. **Goal 2**: Achieve 85% user retention after 3 months of active use
3. **Goal 3**: Support both individual and small business use cases (up to 50 employees)
4. **Goal 4**: Integrate with at least 10 common data sources (banking, utilities, travel)

## The Product Is/Is Not, Does/Does Not

### The Product **IS**

| The Product **IS** | The Product **IS NOT** |
| :--- | :--- |
| A carbon footprint tracking and reduction app | A general environmental awareness platform |
| Automated data collection from multiple sources | Manual data entry only |
| Personalized with actionable recommendations | Generic carbon calculators |
| Designed for individuals and small businesses | Enterprise carbon management solution |
| Mobile-first with web companion | Desktop-only application |
| Free tier with premium features | Fully paid service |

### The Product **DOES**

| The Product **DOES** | The Product **DOES NOT** |
| :--- | :--- |
| Track carbon emissions from daily activities | Sell carbon offsets directly |
| Provide personalized reduction recommendations | Handle carbon credit trading |
| Visualize progress with charts and goals | Connect to government reporting systems |
| Compare user footprint to averages and targets | Provide legal compliance certification |
| Send reminders and tips for reduction | Manage corporate sustainability reporting |
| Integrate with banking and utility APIs | Replace existing business ERP systems |

## EcoTrack Vision Summary

EcoTrack is designed to make carbon footprint tracking simple, automated, and actionable. By connecting to users' existing data sources and providing personalized recommendations, EcoTrack empowers individuals and small businesses to make meaningful environmental impact without overwhelming complexity.

The EcoTrack platform helps users:
- Understand their carbon footprint across multiple categories
- Track progress toward reduction goals
- Receive actionable, personalized tips
- Visualize environmental impact over time
