## GreenSpace Brainstorming Insights

### Key User Needs Identified
1. **Prevention**: Keep plants healthy before problems occur
2. **Education**: Learn proper care for each plant type
3. **Convenience**: Easy reminders that fit into daily routine
4. **Community**: Connect with other plant parents
5. **Diagnosis**: Identify and fix problems quickly

### Opportunity Areas
1. **Visual Identification**: Make plant care accessible to beginners
2. **Proactive Alerts**: Prevent problems before they happen
3. **Personalized Care**: Tailor recommendations to specific plants
4. **Community Support**: Learn from other plant enthusiasts
5. **Progress Tracking**: See improvement over time

### User Segments
1. **Beginners**: Need simple guides and reminders
2. **Enthusiasts**: Want detailed tracking and diagnostics
3. **Collectors**: Need to manage large plant collections
4. **Gift Givers**: Looking for plant recommendations
