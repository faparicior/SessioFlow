### User: Alex Johnson (Team Manager)

#### Stages
| Stage | Actions | Thoughts | Feelings | Opportunities |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Create Task** | Opens TaskFlow, creates new task | "What needs to be done this sprint?" | Focused | Quick task creation with templates |

## TaskFlow Journey Maps - Key Insights

### Pain Points Identified
1. **Meeting Fatigue**: Too many synchronous check-ins
2. **Context Switching**: Constant notifications interrupt deep work
3. **Time Zone Friction**: Hard to coordinate across zones
4. **Visibility Gap**: Unclear team progress without asking

### Moments of Truth
1. **Task Creation**: Clarity sets expectations
2. **Async Update**: Feeling heard without meeting
3. **Progress Dashboard**: Confidence in team alignment

### Emotional Journey
- **Manager**: Focused → Thoughtful → Relieved → Satisfied → Confident
- **Developer**: Ready → Confident → Helpful → Accomplished → Open
- **Team**: Neutral → Efficient → Informed → Collaborative → Proactive
