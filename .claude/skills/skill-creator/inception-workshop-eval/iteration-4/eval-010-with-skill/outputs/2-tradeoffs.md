## 3. Consensus Reasoning

**Why Usability is #1:**
TaskFlow targets remote teams who are already overwhelmed. If the tool isn't intuitive, adoption will fail regardless of features.

**Key Trade-offs Made:**
- **Usability > Flexibility**: Simplified workflows over customizable complexity
- **Simplicity > Scalability**: Launch with solid core, scale as needed
- **Time to Market > Perfect Architecture**: Get to market first, iterate based on feedback

**Golden Rule Applied:**
Only one check per column ensures forced prioritization and clear decision-making.
