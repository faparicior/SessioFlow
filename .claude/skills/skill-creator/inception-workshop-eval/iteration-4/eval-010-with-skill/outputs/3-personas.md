# Step 3: Personas - TaskFlow

## Primary Persona: Remote Team Manager

### Profile
- **Name**: Alex Johnson
- **Role**: Engineering Manager
- **Age**: 35
- **Team Size**: 12 developers across 4 time zones
- **Tech Savviness**: High

### Goals & Motivations
- Keep team aligned without constant meetings
- Get visibility into project progress
- Reduce meeting fatigue for the team
- Enable flexible working hours

### Frustrations & Pain Points
- Too many status update meetings
- Team members in different time zones miss important discussions
- Hard to track who's working on what
- Existing tools require real-time participation

### Quote
> "I spend more time in meetings than my team does. I need a better way to stay aligned."
