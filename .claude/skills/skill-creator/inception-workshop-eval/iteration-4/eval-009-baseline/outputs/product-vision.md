# EcoTrack - Product Vision

## Overview

EcoTrack is a carbon footprint tracking app for individuals and small businesses.

## Problem

People want to reduce their environmental impact but don't know how.

## Solution

Track carbon emissions and provide recommendations.

## Features

- Carbon tracking
- Recommendations
- Progress visualization

## Target Users

- Individuals
- Small businesses

## Goals

- Reduce carbon footprint
- Increase awareness
