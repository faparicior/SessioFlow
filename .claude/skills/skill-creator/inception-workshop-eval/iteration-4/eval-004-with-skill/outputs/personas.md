# Step 3: Personas - QuickBite

## Primary Persona: Busy Professional

### Profile
- **Name**: Jennifer Martinez
- **Role**: Marketing Manager
- **Age**: 32
- **Location**: Urban area
- **Tech Savviness**: High

### Goals & Motivations
- Order healthy meals quickly during busy workdays
- Discover new restaurants without extensive research
- Track spending on food delivery
- Find options that fit tight schedules

### Frustrations & Pain Points
- Limited time to cook after long work hours
- Confusing interfaces on existing delivery apps
- Unclear delivery time estimates
- Limited healthy options in her area

### Behavior Patterns
- Orders 3-4 times per week, mostly lunch and dinner
- Uses QuickBite during commute and lunch breaks
- Reads reviews before trying new restaurants
- Values speed and reliability over price

### Quote
> "I need food that arrives when it says it will, so I can actually take a break during my busy day."

## QuickBite Persona Insights

### Key User Needs
1. **Reliability**: Accurate delivery time estimates
2. **Transparency**: Clear pricing with no hidden fees
3. **Discovery**: Easy way to find new restaurants
4. **Control**: Ability to filter by dietary needs and preferences
5. **Speed**: Fast, intuitive ordering process

### Opportunity Areas
1. **Smart Recommendations**: Personalized suggestions based on order history
2. **Group Ordering**: Easy collaboration for office orders
3. **Nutritional Info**: Detailed information for health-conscious users
4. **Restaurant Dashboard**: Better tools for restaurant owners

### User Journey Touchpoints
- **Discovery**: App store, social media, word of mouth
- **Onboarding**: Quick signup, preference setup
- **Ordering**: Browse, customize, checkout
- **Delivery**: Real-time tracking, notifications
- **Post-order**: Reviews, reordering, loyalty rewards
