# QuickBite - Personas

## User Types

1. **Customers** - People ordering food
2. **Restaurants** - Local eateries offering delivery
3. **Drivers** - People delivering orders

## Customer Persona

- Busy professionals who need quick meals
- Families looking for convenient dinner options
- Health-conscious individuals seeking nutritious options

## Restaurant Persona

- Small local restaurants wanting more customers
- Established chains expanding delivery reach

## Key Needs

- Fast ordering
- Reliable delivery
- Good prices
